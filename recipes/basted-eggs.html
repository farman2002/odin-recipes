Basted Eggs

Description

This recipe produces a delicious, simple egg with satisfying, set whites and a creamy, runny yolk. It's similar to a poached egg, but more flavorful and less fussy.

Per Serving: 296 calories; fat 27.2g; cholesterol 417.8mg; sodium 583.1mg; carbohydrates 1g; protein 12.8g.

Ingredients

  • 1½ tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch flaky sea salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Crack eggs and let it cook
  3. Add water
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low
  5. Remove lid and transfer eggs to a serving plate
