Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells

Description

These chicken Alfredo stuffed shells are served in a rich and decadent Alfredo sauce that is creamy and cheesy. The cheese pull when serving is amazing.

Per Serving: 769 calories; fat 54.5g; cholesterol 200.9mg; sodium 1078.9mg; carbohydrates 32.1g; protein 38g.

Ingredients

  • 30 each jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¾ cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. cook the pasta shells according to package directions for al dente, 9 to 11 minutes
  3. Season chicken breasts on both sides with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  4. Combine 2 cups cream and butter in a deep, large skillet
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and golden, 20 to 25 minutes
