Cookies and Cream Brownies

Description

Soft and gooey on the inside, crisp on the outside dark chocolate brownies are topped with a layer of creamy topping and crushed cookies in this decadent but easy dessert.

Per Serving: 328 calories; fat 17.4g; cholesterol 57.8mg; sodium 183.5mg; carbohydrates 42.4g; protein 3.6g.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup high-quality unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix sugar, flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder together in a mixing bowl
  3. Pour 1/2 of the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth out with a spatula
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are brown and center is set, 30 to 35 minutes
  5. Remove pan from the oven and set on a wire rack. Let brownies cool completely, at least 30 minutes.
